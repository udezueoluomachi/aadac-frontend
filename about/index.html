aaDAC is a nonprofit network building and empowering a community of artists across Africa and the Diaspora with the inspiration and information they need to affect critical change within their local communities.

#1 Community For Visual Artists Working Across Africa And the Diaspora

Connect With the Right Artistic Network

Connecting emerging and established artists through a cooperative artistic network, offering exclusive benefits to be part of a growing community of artists commited to cultivating productive artistic connection within their local spaces.

Capacity Building

Building visual artists through a productive and resilient network, our nonprofit projects work to empower visual artists as cultural agents capable to affect critical change within their local communities.

Grow Your Artistic Network

Expand your art practice with the right resources, tools , information and inspiration you need to grow your artistic network, both locally and internationally.

Inspire A Global Audience

Promote your work to the right audience globally. Our nonprofit network is constantly working to partner with an international network of writers, curators and galleries, from art institutions to academia, through our community driven projects and engagements, whch include publication of art news and innovating programs; including access and linkage to resources and platforms, for building a sustainable community for artists to be change makers within their local communities.
