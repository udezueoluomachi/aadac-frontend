At aaDAC, our artistic community matter to the world, from individuals to civil society. We are a media nonprofit network building a community for artists driving social change across Africa and the Diaspora; empowering artists with the inspiration, resource and information they need to affect critical change within their local communities around the world.

For us to continue our work, we depend on the goodwill of supporters and donors from around the world.

Through our community driven projects and engagement, which include publication of art news and innovating programs; access and linkage to resources and art platforms, building a community for various stakeholders while re-informing how culture works, communities and the institutions that keep them can be a space for social instigation and change the interface of art and contemporary society.

In a world saturated with new media technologies, with the Internet and Artificial Intelligence (AI) playing critical role in how we interact and engage with real and imagined world, cultural work takes on an interface to alter how we see the world.

With dislocations caused by global pandemics and international crisis, cultural work and communities that make them call for sustainability, a resilient space where artists can ideate and imagine new realities, linking communities with institutions that nuture them while building sustainable communities for artists to become changemakers. While our work mediate the role of art on society, our nonprofit media focus on the core mission; to empower and inspire a community of artists produce the work needed to affect critical change within their communities.

Culturally resilient communities including artists, galleries and art organisations continually count on us, and we, in turn, rely on your donation.

Your donation will help us in strengthening our international network, partnering with writers, curators and galleries, from art institutions to academia to amplify our community reach around the world.

Through this, we will develop programs, projects and critical publications that empower artists and in turn inspire a global audience.
